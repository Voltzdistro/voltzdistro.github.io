Voltzdistro Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 26, 2025

Voltzdistro Inc. ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to maintaining your privacy and keeping your information secure. We don't believe in bloated data collection, intrusive analytics, or shady practices. Our mission is to build secure, efficient tools — not to track or exploit you.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect (if any), how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. What We Collect

  • Anonymous usage data (e.g., page loads, browser type) via privacy-respecting tools.
  • User-submitted data (e.g., contact messages, feedback, bug reports).
  • Download events (count only, no IPs or identities unless explicitly submitted).

We do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers without your permission.
  • Location data.
  • Background tracking or fingerprinting.

2. Cookies

We use no third-party tracking cookies.
If cookies are used at all, they are strictly functional — to store UI preferences or session settings.

3. How We Use Your Data

If any data is collected:

  • It's used only to improve the performance, reliability, and security of our tools.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or third parties.

4. Data Retention

  • We store minimal logs and wipe anything sensitive after a short retention period.
  • User-submitted content (e.g., bug reports or feedback) may be kept for internal use and improvement.

5. Security

  • All form submissions are protected.
  • Data is encrypted where applicable.
  • No bloatware = fewer attack surfaces.

6. Your Rights

If you ever contact us and want your submitted data deleted or reviewed, we’ll handle it.
Just email: voltzdistro@gmail.com

7. Third-Party Tools

Some tools or links may open GitHub, WhatsApp, or third-party sites. We don't control their privacy — check their policies separately.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy when necessary. If we do, the Last Updated date will change at the top.
